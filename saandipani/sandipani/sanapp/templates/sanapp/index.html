About Our Company

High Range of Exploring Protection

Saandipani Educational Group is a top overseas education consultancy in Nepal, guiding students in studying abroad. Saandipani Educational Group aims to empower students and provide them with a friendly and supportive environment to realize their goals of studying abroad.

They assist with applications, visas, college selection, and test prep (IELTS, PTE, TOEFL) for countries like the UK, USA, Canada, and Australia. With personalized support, they help even average-profile students achieve their study-abroad dreams.
